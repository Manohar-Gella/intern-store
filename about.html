About Gella Job Info

Welcome to Gella Job Info, an open-source project dedicated to providing career resources for computer science students and enthusiasts.

As a computer science student myself, I created this platform to store and organize various career options within the field of computer science. Whether you're exploring potential career paths, seeking internships, or planning your next career move, Gella Job Info is here to assist you.

This project aims to centralize information about different job roles, industries, and companies relevant to computer science professionals. Feel free to explore the curated collection of career options and contribute your own findings to help others in their career journey.
