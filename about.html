About Us

Welcome to Gella Intern Info, your go-to portal for career options and insights for computer science students and enthusiasts!

This site is developed as a pet open-source project for my slef but later decided to help fellow students and professionals in the field of computer science find internship and job opportunities.

Whether you are a student looking for internships or a professional seeking new career paths, Gella Intern Info is here to provide valuable resources and information to guide you on your journey.

Feel free to explore the site, contribute to the project, and share it with others who might benefit from the information provided. Together, we can build a supportive community for career development in the tech industry.
